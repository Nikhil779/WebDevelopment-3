Know About Us

Minions is a 2015 American 3D computer-animated comedy film, serving as a spin-off prequel to the Despicable Me franchise. Produced by Illumination Entertainment for Universal Pictures, it was directed by Pierre Coffin and Kyle Balda, and produced by Chris Meledandri and Janet Healy.

Who Created Us

The Flying Monkeys never got their own movie. Nor did the Imperial Stormtroopers. Nor did Rosa Klebb, Oddjob, or Jaws—the Moonrakersidekick, not the shark. You know who did get their own movie? The Minions, which, by Hollywood law, makes them the greatest movie henchmen of all time.

Read Me

The Minions are small, yellow henchmen shaped like pill capsules. They are depicted as being roughly one-third to one-half the height of humans but they were later revealed. They have one or two eyes, and their irises are almost always brown (except for Bob, who has one green and one brown eye).
